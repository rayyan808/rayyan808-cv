Background

I am a British 21 year old currently residing in the city of Groningen for the duration of my studies but I also wish to continue my professional work life here. I consider myself to be easily embedded into new teams, I can convey ideas and coding concepts in a clear precise manner and I am able to completely dedicate myself to projects and tune into a code binge. Dutch is not my strongsuit but I am in the process of studying it (If surrounded by Dutch speakers in my work environment, I hope to learn at a faster pace). I have had the passion of problem-solving and coding in particuler from a very young age. From the age of 13, I was already familiar with the lifestyle of a programmer: All day coding marathons consisting of bug fixes, testing, bug fixes, testing! Most of my adoloscent years were spent creating/modding games using C# or C (XNA Framework), Meddling with PHP-SQL based websites for friends and family and later on; Projects for Private Clients in the form of Web Applications. I enjoy learning new languages and understanding the syntactical structure behind them and also wish to persue my interests in Machine Learning further down the line.

As a Dynamic Computer Scientist, I understand the theory and logic behind Computations which aides me in my learning of Software Syntax and a deeper understanding of Algorthmic Problems than a common Software Engineer. I pride myself in being adaptable and flexible, I can adjust to new requirements and technologies. I am currently completing my final year of Bachelor Studies at The University of Groningen and seek to be a productive resource to a team of like-minded individuals.

I am familiar with languages such as C#, C, Haskell, Python, Node.JS, Java, PHP
I am also familiar with SQL, noSQL, Apache, React, Express, SSH, OOP, Functional Programming, Continous Integration, Task Management, Multi-Threading (OpenAcc, OpenMP, Pthreads)
