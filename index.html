Background

I am a Bachelor of Science graduate in the field of Computing Science, in my final year I opted to work on developing my own software coined the 'HOZK-Voter' Zero-Knowledge Proof based voting system that utilizes Ethereum Blockchain technology. This system is the first of it's kind and (hopefully) has opened doors towards future research in blockchain technologies coupling SNARK circuit usage as well as promoting a truly democratic voting procedure. I have had the passion of the blockchain architecture to the point I co-patented Equilibratetuxn, a start-up based on physical sensory suits harnessing blockchain technology to create the worlds first sports economy ( We are currently looking for investors ;) contact: Me: rayyan@equilibratetuxn.com or Eric: eric@equilibratetuxn.com))!
Even my adoloscent years were spent creating/modding games using C# or C (XNA Framework), Meddling with PHP-SQL based websites for friends and family and later on; Projects for Private Clients in the form of Web Applications. I enjoy learning new languages and understanding the syntactical structure behind them and also wish to pursue my interests in Machine Learning further down the line.


As a Dynamic Computer Scientist, I understand the theory and logic behind Computations which aides me in my learning of Software Syntax and a deeper understanding of Algorithmic Problems than a common Software Engineer. I pride myself in being adaptable and flexible, I can adjust to new requirements and technologies.
